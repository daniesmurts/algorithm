# Project Brief: «Алгоритм» (Algorithm) — Systemic Construction & Renovation

## 1. Project Overview
**Company Name:** Алгоритм (Algorithm)  
**Industry:** Premium residential and commercial construction, high-end renovation, and engineering.  
**Core Value Prop:** "Systemic precision in construction." Moving away from "creative chaos" toward a strict, mathematical, and engineered technological process.  
**Target Audience:** High-net-worth individuals and business owners in Kazan looking for "turnkey" solutions with total transparency and engineering excellence.

## 2. Visual Identity & Brand Assets
**Design System:** "Quiet Engineering" — Minimalist, precise, high-end.  
**Color Palette:**
- **Primary:** Deep Green (`#26943b`) — used for CTAs and key accents.
- **Backgrounds:** Off-white (`#faf9f9`) for Light Mode; Deep Charcoal/Black for Dark Mode.
- **Typography:** Professional sans-serif (Hanken Grotesk) with a focus on hierarchy and legibility.

**Official Assets:**
- **Primary Logo:** Horizontal wordmark (`АЛГОРИТМ  логотип 1.png`).
- **Symbolic Mark:** The "A" glyph (`АЛГОРИТМ  логот доп 1.png` - white/transparent).
- **Style Reference:** Minimalist architectural photography featuring clean lines, concrete, wood, and soft natural lighting.

## 3. Site Structure (Single Page Application / Long-read)
1. **Hero Section:** High-impact value proposition ("Repair as an Exact Science").
2. **Services:** Structured list or grid (Design, Turnkey Renovation, Engineering, etc.).
3. **The "Algorithm" Approach:** Алексей Волков (Founder) section highlighting the 150-point checklist and technological process.
4. **Portfolio (Cases):** High-quality project cards leading to immersive galleries.
5. **Methodology:** Step-by-step process (Analysis → Project → Construction → Handover).
6. **Lead Gen:** "Discuss your renovation" / "Calculate your algorithm" forms.
7. **Footer:** Contact details, social links, and brand anchors.

## 4. Key Pages & Interactivity
### Main Landing Page
- High-fidelity desktop experience with responsive design.
- Integration of premium motion and transitions to reflect "precision."

### Project Gallery Page
- **Purpose:** Deep dive into specific completed projects.
- **Features:** 
    - Full-screen imagery.
    - Technical specifications (Area, Duration, Materials).
    - "Technical Accents" highlighting specific engineering solutions (e.g., bio-dynamic lighting, climate control).

## 5. Technical Requirements
- **Language:** Russian (Primary).
- **Device Support:** Desktop-first (High-resolution), with mobile responsiveness.
- **Performance:** Optimized image loading for high-resolution architectural photography.
- **Design Principles:** 
    - **Fidelity:** Adherence to the 0.5mm precision brand promise.
    - **Consistency:** Uniform use of brand colors and typography across all sub-pages.
